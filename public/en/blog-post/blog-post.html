Beatnik. (2021-11-01)

It is surprising, perhaps, that in the age of the always-on, always-recording internet, that the evolution of our language so often eludes precise study. Exact origins of our daily expressions, slang, and neologisms are only vaguely understood. General linguistic pathways, or intersections cultural moments that come and pass.
