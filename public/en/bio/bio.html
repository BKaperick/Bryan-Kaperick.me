Bio

Hello, I’m Bryan - a 29 year old guy living in Lille, France. I enjoy working on problems in computational mathematics, data science/engineering, and numerical analysis. Here in France, I enjoy walking around parks and cities, practicing my French with strangers in bars, and rock climbing.
