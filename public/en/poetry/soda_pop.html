Soda pop – October 2024

I heard it all at the countertop:
A rubbery mix.
The pangs of soda water unspriz'd.
The clank
      and clank
            and clank.
Flat taps:
   a rhythm to suscitate
all those syrupy drips unsipped
Only fizzled-out sparks emit
Better seal it up quick
Lick
   it
      and
         spit.
Bottle it all up,
and crank
      and crank
            and crank
the nozzle shut.
